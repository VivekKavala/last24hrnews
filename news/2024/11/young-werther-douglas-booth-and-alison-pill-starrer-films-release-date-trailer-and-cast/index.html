Young Werther: Douglas Booth and Alison Pill starrer film’s release date, trailer and cast

Lionsgate’s upcoming film Young Werther, based on Goethe’s classic novel The Sorrows of Young Werther, has unveiled a new trailer.

Directed by José Lourenço, this modern adaptation explores the complexities of unrequited love with a fresh, contemporary setting.

Trailer The Young Werther trailer introduces the main character, portrayed by Douglas Booth.

Known for his roles in That Dirty Black Bag and Unwelcome, Booth plays a young man deeply in love with Charlotte, played by Alison Pill.

Charlotte, however, is engaged to another man, portrayed by Patrick J.

Adams.

As Booth’s character navigates his feelings, the story unfolds, showcasing his attempts to win Charlotte’s affection against all odds.

Release DateJosé Lourenço, the director of Young Werther, is stepping into new territory with this film.

Lourenço, known for his previous work in short films, brings Goethe’s story into the present-day world.

With its premiere at the Toronto International Film Festival, the film has already captured the interest of critics and filmmakers.

The official release is set for December 3, available both in theaters and on demand.
