Inside Aishwarya Arjun’s Dreamy Parisian Holiday

Aishwarya Arjun, daughter of renowned actor Arjun Sarja, made her debut as a leading lady in Kannada cinema with the film Prema Baraha in 2018.

Aishwarya, who has worked in both Sandalwood and Kollywood films, has not appeared in any recent movie projects, leading fans to wonder about her career plans.

Recently, she was in the spotlight due to rumours and discussions surrounding her marriage, adding to the curiosity about her personal life.

Aishwarya Arjun and Tamil actor Umapathy Ramaiah recently tied the knot in a traditional ceremony.

The couple exchanged vows in an intimate ceremony at Arjun’s Sri Yoga Anjaneyar Temple in Gerugambakkam, Chennai.

Their grand wedding, held just a few months ago, was attended by numerous celebrities and politicians, who extended their heartfelt wishes to the newlyweds.

Aishwarya, known for her active presence on social media, frequently shares photos and videos, giving her followers a glimpse into her life and updates about her journey.

Actress Aishwarya Arjun, currently vacationing in Paris, has shared some stunning photos on her Instagram.

Her choice to write the title in Kannada delighted many fans, though some netizens playfully pointed out minor misspellings in her post.

One follower commented, appreciating her use of Kannada and noting that Paris truly is romantic.

View this post on Instagram A post shared by Aishwarya Arjun (@aishwaryaarjun) Shortly after posting the photo, Aishwarya Arjun quickly corrected the spelling error, updating the caption to accurately read “Paris Romance.

” Fans applauded her attentiveness, praising her for promptly fixing the mistake.

Many supporters commented that other actresses who seem to have distanced themselves from their Kannada roots could take inspiration from Aishwarya’s pride in the language.

Aishwarya Arjun made her film debut in 2013 with Pattathu Yaanai, where she starred alongside Vishal.

She later took on her first leading role in 2018 with the Kannada film Prema Baraha.

This movie was also released in Tamil under the title Sollividava, with certain scenes reshot to suit the Tamil audience.

Despite the anticipation, the film didn’t achieve the level of success initially expected in either language.

Her husband, Umapathy Ramaiah, also has a budding career in cinema.

Entering the industry in 2017, he has appeared in just four films so far, but his performances have managed to capture audience attention, gradually building him a fan base in Tamil cinema.

It appears that Umapathy Ramaiah and Aishwarya Arjun had been in love for quite some time before they decided to tie the knot.

After receiving a warm approval from their families, the couple happily celebrated their marriage.

Now, they are cherishing their new journey together as a married couple.

In 2021, Aishwarya participated in the Survivor reality show hosted by her father, actor Arjun, on Zee Tamil, where Umapathy also appeared.

This show became the starting point of their friendship, and it was during this time that Umapathy grew closer to Arjun’s family.

Over time, this friendship blossomed into a romantic relationship.

When the two decided to marry, their families met at the Hanuman temple, which was built by Arjun himself, to discuss the marriage plans.

Following this, Aishwarya and Umapathy were married on June 10 in a ceremony held at the Hanuman temple in Chennai, surrounded by close friends and family.
