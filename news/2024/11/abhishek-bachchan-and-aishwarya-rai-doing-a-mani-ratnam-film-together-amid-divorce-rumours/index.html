Abhishek Bachchan And Aishwarya Rai Doing A Mani Ratnam Film Together Amid Divorce Rumours?

Rumours about Abhishek Bachchan and Aishwarya Rai Bachchan’s marriage have been circulating online, but recent news suggests a fresh chapter may be unfolding for the couple – this time on screen! Renowned filmmaker Mani Ratnam is reportedly planning a new Hindi film, set to star the iconic “Guru” couple, Abhishek and Aishwarya.

This potential collaboration is generating excitement, hinting at a reunion that could squash any speculations surrounding their relationship.

In 2007, Mani Ratnam’s Guru brought the couple together on screen just as they were about to tie the knot.

The chemistry between them impressed audiences, and Ratnam later cast them again in Raavan.

According to a TimesNow.

com report, the filmmaker has finally found a compelling story to reunite the Bachchan duo for a third film.

This would be Abhishek’s fourth collaboration with Ratnam, having first worked with him in Yuva.

Reflecting on his deep bond with Ratnam, Abhishek once shared with Zoom, “When he had come to our home for the first time to sign me for Yuva, I thought he had come to sign Pa (father Amitabh Bachchan).

When I got to know it was me he wanted I was beyond ecstatic.

Any actor would give a limb to work with Mani.

I take great pride in the fact that he found me worthy of his cinema thrice, so far, and that too without losing any limbs.

” Despite recent whispers of trouble in their marriage, Abhishek and Aishwarya have faced such rumours before.

Back in 2014, similar claims surfaced, but Abhishek dismissed them with humour, tweeting, “Ok.

So I believe I’m getting divorced.

Thanks for letting me know! Will you let me know when I’m getting remarried too?” With their electrifying on-screen presence and unwavering fan support, this reunion with Mani Ratnam could be an iconic moment for Abhishek and Aishwarya, rekindling the magic they’ve created in their past films.
