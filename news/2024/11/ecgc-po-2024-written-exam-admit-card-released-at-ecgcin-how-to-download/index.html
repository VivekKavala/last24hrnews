ECGC PO 2024 Written Exam Admit Card Released at ecgc.in, How To Download

The Export Credit Guarantee Corporation of India (ECGC) Ltd has released the ECGC Probationary Officer (PO) Admit Card 2024.

Registered candidates can now download their hall tickets from the official website, ecgc.

in.

To access the ECGC PO admit card, candidates need to enter their registration number and password on the portal.

Please note that the admit card will remain available on the website until November 16, 2024.

The ECGC PO 2024 written exam is scheduled for November 16.

The test will consist of two parts: Part 1, a Multiple-Choice Question (MCQ) paper worth 200 marks and Part 2, a Descriptive Paper worth 40 marks.

The objective section will cover topics including reasoning ability, English language skills, computer knowledge, general awareness, and numerical aptitude, with a total duration of 140 minutes for this section.

On the exam day, all candidates are required to bring a printed copy of their admit card along with a valid photo ID to the examination hall.

ECGC PO 2024 Admit Card: Steps To Download Step 1: Go to ECGC’s official website at ecgc.

in.

Step 2: On the homepage, look for and click on the ‘ECGC PO Admit Card 2024’ link.

Step 3: As a new window opens, candidates must enter their necessary details (Registration Number, password, and captcha code).

Then click ‘Login’.

Step 4: The ECGC PO 2024 Admit Card will appear on the screen.

Step 5: Check all the details on the admit card and download the page.

The results for the ECGC PO 2024 written exam are expected to be announced between December 16 and 31, 2024.

Candidates who pass the exam will be shortlisted for the interview round, which is likely to take place in January or February 2025.

This recruitment drive aims to fill 40 Probationary Officer vacancies at the organization.
