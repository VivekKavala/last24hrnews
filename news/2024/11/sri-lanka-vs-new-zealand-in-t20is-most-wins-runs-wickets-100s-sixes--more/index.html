Sri Lanka Vs New Zealand In T20Is: Most Wins, Runs, Wickets, 100s, Sixes & More

Sri Lanka will face New Zealand in a two-match T20I series.

The series opener is scheduled to take place at Rangiri Dambulla International Stadium in Dambulla on Saturday (November 9), and the second match will also be played at the same venue on Sunday (November 10).

Chraith Aalanka will lead the Sri Lankan team in the home series, whereas the Kiwis will play under the leadership of all-rounder Mitchell Santner.

Will Young, Glenn Phillips, and Ish Sodhi, who played in the recently concluded third Test against India at Wankhede Stadium in Mumbai, are part of the T20I side and would like to come up with a big show against the Islanders to help the Kiwis win the series.

Here’s a look at top performers and record holders in 23 T20Is played so far between New Zealand and Sri Lanka: Most wins: New Zealand (13 runs in 23 T20Is).

Biggest win (by wickets): New Zealand beat Sri Lanka by 9 wickets (60 balls remaining) in Auckland on January 10, 2016.

Smallest win: New Zealand beat Sri Lanka by 3 runs in Colombo on September 2, 2009, and in Mount Maunganui on January 7, 2016.
