Liverpool sweeps the German champions in the Champions League

Luis Diaz scored three hat-trick goals in Liverpool’s match against Bayer Leverkusen, leading the Reds to a sweeping victory over the German champions with a score of 4-0 in the Champions League.

At Anfield Stadium, Liverpool taught the “Bundesliga” champion a harsh lesson under the leadership of its Spanish coach, Xavi Alonso, who in turn returned to the “Reds” stronghold after a long absence, in the fourth round of the Champions League.

Diaz opened the scoring in the 60th minute for Liverpool, before his teammate Jacobo doubled the lead in the 64th minute.

In the 83rd minute, the Colombian international returned to Leverkusen’s net, scoring his second and his team’s third goal, before returning a third time to declare himself champion of Tuesday night’s Champions League.
