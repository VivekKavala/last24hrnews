Yeh Kaali Kaali Ankhein 2 Trailer: Gurmeet Choudhary Joins Anchal Singh, Tahir Raj Bhasin In Netflix Series

Fans of the romantic crime thriller show Yeh Kaali Kaali Ankhein, which released on Netflix in 2022, are in for a treat! The show is returning with a second season soon, and the makers have now shared the trailer of the dark thriller series.

Gurmeet Choudhary has joined Tahir Raj Bhasin, Aanchal Singh and Shweta Tripathi, Saurabh Shukla, Arunoday Singh, and others in the show, and the trailer looks promising! Netflix shared the trailer of Yeh Kaali Kaali Ankhein season 2 on Wednesday morning, along with the caption, “Mohabbat nahi, ab junoon ka season aa gaya hain Watch the twisted tale of love, passion, and betrayal, Yeh Kaali Kaali Ankhein Season 2, out 22 November, only on Netflix! #YehKaaliKaaliAnkheinOnNetflix.

” The first season ended on a cliffhanger, with Anchal Singh’s character Purva being abducted by the killer.

If the trailer is anything to go by, the second season will definitely be more thriller and darker than before, and will keep you at the edge of your seat.

Gurmeet Choudhary will be seen as the new character Guru, and his entry has raised the stakes in this tole of obsessive love.

Check out the trailer below! View this post on Instagram A post shared by Netflix India (@netflix_in) As soon as the trailer dropped, fans expressed their excitement for Yeh Kaali Kaali Ankhein Season 2.

While one fan commented, “Whoooooo!!!! FINALLLY GC’S DEBUT ON NETFLIX SHAAAANNDAAR,” another comment read, “Counting days to watch it.

” Sidharth Sengupta of Edgestorm Ventures Pvt Ltd has produced written and directed Yeh Kaali Kaali Ankhein Season 2.

The powerhouse cast of this series includes Tahir Raj Bhasin, Shweta Tripathi, Anchal Singh, Saurabh Shukla, Arunoday Singh, Varun Badola, Gurmeet Choudhary, Brijendra Kala, Anant Joshi, Surya Sharma, Shashi Verma, Anjuman Saxena, and Hetal Gada.

It will release on Netflix on 22nd November, 2024.

The first season of Yeh Kaali Kaali Ankhein was released on Netflix on 14th January, 2022, over two years ago.

The show left viewers impressed with its engaging storyline and stellar cast.
