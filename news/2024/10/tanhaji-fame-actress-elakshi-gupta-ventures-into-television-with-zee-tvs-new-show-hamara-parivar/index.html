Tanhaji Fame Actress Elakshi Gupta Ventures into Television with Zee TV’s New Show ‘Hamara Parivar’

Elakshi Gupta, known for her captivating performances in films and music videos, like Tanhaji, Love You Shankar, and many more, has now made her debut in the television industry with Zee TV’s new show, Hamara Parivar.

This exciting venture marks a unique turn in her career as the actress steps into a one-of-a-kind family-based interactive show, offering a refreshing concept that promises to engage both the actors and their fans personally.

Hamara Parivar is not your typical television program.

It centers around the theme of family and connection, featuring actors and their “parivar” (family) in a one-on-one heart conversation where they not only share stories but also answer questions from their fanbase.

The show is designed to break the bridge between celebrities and viewers, allowing fans to get to know their favorite actors in a more intimate way.

One of the standout features of the show is its interactive set tours and celebrity visits, where audiences get to explore the Hamara Parivar sets and share their thoughts on various ZEE TV shows.

Speaking about her involvement in the show, Elakshi expressed her excitement: “This is a very unique show that intrigued me.

This is my parivar and I am glad that I get to portray Sakshi.

It’s a show that is about breaking the gap between the actors and their fans, making us feel like one big family.

We ask questions that fans have always wanted to ask, bringing a whole new dynamic to the usual celebrity-audience interaction.

The audience gets to know them way more personally via the TV show itself, especially those who are not very Techie with Social Media they get to know their per.

I’m thrilled by the positive response we’ve been getting and I am really looking forward to this journey.

” Elakshi’s journey into television with such a unique concept speaks volumes about her versatility and willingness to explore new horizons.

With Hamara Parivar, she not only captivates viewers but also brings something new to the table—an interactive experience that breaks the conventional boundaries of celebrity shows.

We can’t wait to see how Elakshi continues to conquer new heights in her career, and with such an engaging start in television, the actress certainly has a long and successful journey ahead.
