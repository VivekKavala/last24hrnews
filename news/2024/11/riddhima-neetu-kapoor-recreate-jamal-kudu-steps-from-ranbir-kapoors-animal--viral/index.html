Riddhima, Neetu Kapoor Recreate Jamal Kudu Steps From Ranbir Kapoor's Animal | VIRAL

Ranbir Kapoor gained success with his film Animal.

The Sandeep Reddy Vanga directorial became one of the most talked about movies of last years and among the highest grossing movies of all time.

However, Bobby Deol’s entry song Jamal Kudu became an internet sensation and continued to be a top chart buster.

Now Riddhima and mother Neetu Kapoor were seen dancing to the same.

Viral video of Riddhima Kapoor and Neetu Kapoor dancing to Jamal KuduRiddhima Kapoor took to her Instagram handle and shared a post in which she is seen dancing to the popular track Jamal Kudu and was joined by her mother Neetu Kapoor.

Along with the video, she wrote in the caption, “Good vibes happen on the dance floor only with my mummy”.

In the clip, Riddhima Kapoor can be seen grooving to Jamal Kudu with plate on top and mother Neetu joins her.

She looked stunning in a long black skirt with a shimmery maroon top, while Neetu exudes grace in a white ensemble.

View this post on Instagram A post shared by Riddhima Kapoor Sahni (RKS) (@riddhimakapoorsahniofficial) Fans couldn’t get enough of their moves and took to comment section to appreciate the duo.

One user wrote, “Hahahhahaha fabbbbbbb”.

Another user wrote, “Without Neetu aunty there is no fun”.

“Mumma n daughter such a cute video”, wrote the third user.

Interesting fact about Jamal KuduIn Animal, Bobby Deol played a mute character in the Ranbir Kapoor-led film Animal.

Despite having no dialogues, Bobby Deol as Abrar Haque received an overwhelmingly positive response.

In a scene from the movie, the actor’s character is preparing for his third marriage when the song plays.

The actor places a glass over his head and dances around, giving rise to the popular step that became viral on social media thereon.

Bobby Deol's Jamal Kudu song from Animal | Source: IMDbIn an interview with Bollywood Spy, Bobby Deol revealed that the idea of getting drunk and dancing with the glass over his head was his idea.

He said, “He (Sandeep Reddy Vanga) had made me listen to the music beforehand.

He found the song from somewhere and told me ‘I will play this in your introduction.

When we started shooting, the choreographer said, ‘You do it.

’ I was like, ‘what will I do?’ I started dancing and he told me, “No, No.

Don’t do it like Bobby Deol.

’ Then I suddenly remembered the time when I was small and we used to go to Punjab .

I remembered how we used to get drunk and keep glasses on our head.

I never understood why we did it.

It suddenly came in my mind and I did that.

Sandeep liked it.

” Animal Hit is currently available to stream on Netflix.
