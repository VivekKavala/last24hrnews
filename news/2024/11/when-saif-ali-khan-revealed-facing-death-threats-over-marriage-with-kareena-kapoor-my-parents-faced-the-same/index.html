When Saif Ali Khan Revealed Facing Death Threats Over Marriage With Kareena Kapoor: ‘My Parents Faced The Same’

When Saif Ali Khan and Kareena Kapoor Khan decided to get married, they encountered significant challenges.

Alongside the usual fanfare that surrounds Bollywood’s most high-profile unions, the couple faced severe threats, including death threats and threats against their families.

Nonetheless, the two stood firm in their decision, choosing to go ahead with a private ceremony in October 2012 despite these daunting warnings.

Reflecting on the time, Saif Ali Khan had revealed to Rediff that Kareena’s father, Randhir Kapoor, had received anonymous letters filled with threats, claiming that the family would be harmed, and even suggesting that their wedding venue could be targeted.

However, Saif remained undeterred, drawing strength from his family’s history.

He recalled that his parents, Mansoor Ali Khan Pataudi and Sharmila Tagore, had also faced similar resistance during their interfaith marriage in the 1960s.

Saif explained, “Some people were not happy with us getting married.

My father-in-law had received some letters from anonymous people, claiming they will harm our family and bomb the place.

” But these threats didn’t intimidate him.

Saif remained unfazed, adding, “Making threats and carrying them out are two different things.

” Sharmila Tagore, in a previous conversation with Mojo Story, had also shared memories of the challenges she and Saif’s father, Mansoor Ali Khan, faced.

During their wedding in Kolkata, her family received telegrams with threats, ominously stating that “bullets shall speak.

” While Tiger’s (Mansoor Ali Khan’s) family grew increasingly worried, Sharmila recounted that they moved forward, holding a memorable wedding where, fortunately, “nothing untoward happened.

” This legacy of resilience in the face of adversity seems to have inspired Saif and Kareena as well, providing a sense of stability and perspective during their own trials.

Saif Ali Khan and Kareena Kapoor Khan’s marriage is a beautiful testament to their unwavering commitment to one another and to overcoming obstacles together.

The couple first met on the sets of the film Tashan in 2008, sparking an instant connection that evolved over the years into a deep, abiding relationship.

As one of Bollywood’s most iconic couples, Saif and Kareena—often affectionately known as “Saifeena”—embody modern romance while respecting the deep-rooted traditions of their respective families.

Their 2012 wedding, conducted in a simple yet elegant style, was a blend of traditional and contemporary values, honoring both Saif’s Nawabi heritage and Kareena’s illustrious Kapoor lineage.

Their union has been celebrated not only for its cultural symbolism but also for its warmth, intimacy, and authenticity.

From raising two children, Taimur and Jehangir, to balancing demanding careers and family life, Saif and Kareena’s journey has been a masterclass in partnership and resilience.

Today, they remain one of Bollywood’s most respected couples, inspiring fans with their down-to-earth values, steadfast support for each other, and a love story that transcends the silver screen.
