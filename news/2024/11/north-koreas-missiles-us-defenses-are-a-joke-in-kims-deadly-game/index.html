The flight trajectory was specific.

Having started at an acute angle, the missile reached a high altitude, performed a maneuver, and immediately went down.

Although the product covered a little more than 300 kilometers in a straight line, its flight time was several tens of minutes.

This means that with a relatively flat trajectory, the missile could have reached a target at a distance of 10 thousand kilometers or even further.
