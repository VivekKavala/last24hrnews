Pushpa 2 Cast Fees: Allu Arjun's Salary 30 Times More Than Rashmika Mandanna, Fahadh Faasil

Allu Arjun and Rashmika Mandanna ’s Pushpa 2 is just a month away and fans are eagerly waiting for this movie.

Pushpa 2 which is releasing in theatres this year is one of the most highly anticipated films.

As per reports, the film which has made profits even before the film’s release.

How much the cast of the film are being paid?Reportedly, Pushpa 2 has already brought huge numbers even before its release.

The film has already entered ₹1000 crore club with its release and rights.

Rumours are rife that Allu Arjun is the highest paid actor with a fee of ₹300 crore.

While the female lead Rashmika Mandanna will earn ₹10 crore.

Where as Fahadh Faasil will be making ₹8 crore with the sequel.

File photo of Allu Arjun and Fahadh Faasil | Source: IMDbPushpa 2 to undergo changes in music before release?According to a report in 123 Telugu, there are speculations that Allu Arjun and director Sukumar were not satisfied with Devi Sri Prasad’s background music score for Pushpa 2.

But now there are reports that there will three music composers that is, Thamann, Ajaneesh Loknath and Sam CS which are said to be brought in to compose and background score for this much-awaited Pan-India film.

Poster of Allu Arjun starrer Pushpa 2 | Source: IMDbPushpa 2 is written and directed by Sukumar under his banner Sukumar Writings and produced by Naveen Yerneni and Yalamanchili Ravi Shankar under their Mythri Movie Makers banner.

The cinematography is by Mioslaw Kuba Brozek and music is by Devi Sri Prasad.

The film features Allu Arjun in the titular role, alongside Rashmika Mandanna , Fahadh Faasil, Dhananjaya, Jagadeesh Prathap Bandari, Rao Ramesh, Ajay, Sunil, Anasuya Bharadwaj, Shanmukh and Ajay Ghosh.

Pushpa 2 will release in theatres on December 5.
