Rahul Vaidya And Disha Parmar’s Sweet Moment With Paparazzi Wins Hearts

Singer Rahul Vaidya and actress Disha Parmar are redefining couple goals with their adorable public outings and sweet gestures toward each other.

Recently, the two were spotted enjoying a date and posing for the paparazzi, capturing everyone’s hearts with their down-to-earth charm.

In a now-viral clip, Rahul surprised one of the photographers by revealing he’d be driving his own car instead of relying on a driver.

Disha looked stunning in a sleek black dress, while Rahul kept it cool and casual in a white T-shirt, jeans, and matching white sneakers.

Together, they shared a light-hearted moment with the photographers, showing once again why they’re one of the most beloved celebrity couples around.

The video starts with the couple posing for the photographers.

As Disha begins to get into the car, one of the paparazzi asks, “Who will drive the car?” Disha responds with a smile, saying, “Rahul, who else? I don’t even know how to drive.

” After getting inside the car, Rahul Vaidya, a former Bigg Boss contestant, playfully invites the photographers to join them, adding a touch of humour to the moment.

View this post on Instagram A post shared by Viral Bhayani (@viralbhayani) Rahul Vaidya and Disha Parmar are currently embracing their new chapter as parents, having welcomed their daughter, Navya, in September last year.

Recently, the couple made headlines after both were diagnosed with dengue shortly after returning to Mumbai from Pune.

In an interview with Hindustan Times, Rahul shared his thoughts on when he might have been infected, explaining that he travelled to Pune on September 1 and suspects he was bitten by a mosquito around that time.

He mentioned that symptoms began appearing on the fifth night.

Following his recovery, the singer bought a new 3-BHK apartment in Mumbai’s Bandra for Rs 9 crore.

According to Square Yards, the property has a carpet area of around 3,110 sq.

ft.

The transaction, which was finalised in October 2024, included a stamp duty payment of Rs 56.

37 lakh and a registration fee of Rs 30,000.

On the professional front, Rahul Vaidya was recently seen on the show Laughter Chefs and also released his new single, Jisne Daaru Nahi Pi.

Meanwhile, Disha Parmar was last seen in the popular TV show, Bade Achhe Lagte Hain 2, alongside Nakuul Mehta.

Currently, Disha is taking a break from her career to focus on raising their daughter, Navya.
