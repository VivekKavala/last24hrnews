BLACKPINK Lisa Makes Acting Debut In HBO's The White Lotus Season 3, Teaser Out | Watch

Blackpink’s Lisa is currently making headlines for her performance at an award show or be it her funny antics.

Lisa is a Thai rapper, singer and dancer.

She is a member of the South Korean girl group Blackpink, which made its debut under YG Entertainment in August 16.

Now after gaining success in singing career, she is all set to make her acting debut.

Blackpink’s Lisa to make debut in series?The makers have dropped the teaser of the upcoming series The White Lotus Season 3.

The teaser begins with the focus on a life sized Bronze statue in Thailand which sets the journey.

As the camera pans towards the stunning views and landscapes, soon Blackpink’s Lisa pops up.

She is dressed in a white uniform as a resort manager with her hair neatly tied in a bun and she is seen greeting the guests with a friendly smile on her face.

In the show, her name is Mook, which in Thai means white pearl.

View this post on Instagram A post shared by Max (@streamonmax) As soon as the video was dropped, fans got excited and fans flooded the comment section.

One user wrote, “Lisa from Blackpink is making her acting debut in the third season of HBO's The White Lotus and we can't wait! Born in Thailand, where this season is set.

” Another user wrote, “Lisa looks amazing here”.

“She is representing Thailand”, wrote the third user.

File photo of Lisa in The White Lotus Season 3 | Source: XThe White Lotus 3 will mark Lisa’s acting debut in Hollywood.

She is currently Blackpink's second member to make an appearance in an HBO series.

In June 2023, Jennie (credited as Jennie Ruby Jane) debuted as an actor in the drama The Idol.

All about The White LotusThe first season of the Emmy-winning series The White Lotus was launched in July 2021.

It featured Murray Bartlett, Connie Britton, Jennifer Coolidge, Alexandra Daddario, Jake Lacy, Natasha Rothwell, and Steve Zahn.

The satirical comedy-drama series follows the lives of the staff and guests at the White Lotus, a tropical resort in Hawaii.

File photo of The White Lotus | Source: InstagramThe second season premiered on October 30.

Created, written, and directed by Mike White, it is set at an exclusive Sicilian resort and follows the exploits of various guests and employees over a week.

Initially set up as a limited series, the show was expanded for a sophomore run following rave reviews and an overwhelming response from the audience.
