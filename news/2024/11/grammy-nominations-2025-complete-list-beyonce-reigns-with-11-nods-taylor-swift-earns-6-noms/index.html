Grammy nominations 2025 complete list: Beyonce reigns with 11 nods, Taylor Swift earns 6 noms

The nominations for the 2025 Grammy Awards have been announced, and Beyonce leads the way.

She received 11 nominations, including for album, song, and record of the year for music from “Cowboy Carter,” her much-anticipated country albumPost Malone also received nominations in the country categories, including nods for “I Had Some Help,” which gave collaborator Morgan Wallen his first Grammy nomination.

Other notable nominees include newcomers Sabrina Carpenter and Chappell Roan also scooped up their first nominations.

Billie Eilish snagged several nominations, including for album of the year.

The year’s most dominant artist, Taylor Swift, will also compete for several top prizes.
