anil kapoor comedy movies

Anil Kapoor, who made his debut in 1979, is one of the most popular Bollywood actors to date.

Apart from showcasing his prowess in the drama genre, he has also played various comic roles in his career.

Majnu Bhai, his character from Welcome, and his painting are iconic even today.

In this piece, have a look at some of the best Anil Kapoor comedy movies that will leave you in splits.
