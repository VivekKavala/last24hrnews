Honor Magic 7, Magic 7 Pro With Snapdragon 8 Elite SoCs Launched

Honor Magic 7 and Honor Magic 7 Pro were launched in China on Thursday.

The smartphones are powered by Qualcomm's newest octa-core Snapdragon 8 Elite chipsets paired with up to 16GB of RAM.

They feature triple rear camera units including telephoto shooters and carry 50-megapixel front cameras.

The handsets come with IP68 and IP69 ratings for dust and water resistance.

Both Honor Magic 7 and Magic 7 Pro support 100W wired and 80W wireless fast charging and run on Android 15-based MagicOS 9.
