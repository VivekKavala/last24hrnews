Xiaomi 14T Pro With Dimensity 9300+ SoC Debuts Alongside Xiaomi 14T

Xiaomi 14T and Xiaomi 14T Pro were announced at Xiaomi's global launch event in Berlin on Thursday (September 26).

The Xiaomi 14T series feature 6.

67-inch displays with up to 144Hz refresh rate and 4,000nits brightness.

They're equipped with MediaTek Dimensity chipsets.

The pair flaunts Leica-tuned triple rear cameras matching last year's Xiaomi 13T phones.

The Xiaomi 14T and Xiaomi 14T Pro carry 5,000mAh batteries and are IP68-rated for water and dust protection.

The Xiaomi 14T Pro, being the premium model, gets 50W wireless charging capabilities and a Dimensity 9300+ SoC.
