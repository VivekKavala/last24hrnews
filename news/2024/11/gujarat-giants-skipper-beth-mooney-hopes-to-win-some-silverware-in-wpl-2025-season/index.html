Gujarat Giants skipper Beth Mooney hopes to win “some silverware” in WPL 2025 season

After being retained by the Gujarat Gujarat (GG) during the Women’s Premier League (WPL) 2025 Retentions, skipper Beth Mooney has affirmed the hope to win “some silverware” in the upcoming edition of the T20 tournament.

The Gujarat-based franchise has announced the retention of 14 players ahead of the Women’s Premier League (WPL) 2025 auction on Thursday, featuring a strong blend of international and Indian talent.

Beth Mooney led the list of retained players.
