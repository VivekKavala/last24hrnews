UPSC IFS Mains Exam Admit Card 2024 Released At upsc.gov.in, Steps To Download

The Union Public Service Commission (UPSC) has released the Indian Forest Service (IFS) Mains Admit Card 2024 today, November 14.

Eligible candidates can download their admit cards from the official UPSC website, upsc.

gov.

in, or from upsconline.

nic.

in.

Those appearing for the exam will need their login credentials such as application number and date of birth to download their hall tickets.

It is recommended that candidates download and print their e-Admit Cards as soon as they are available.

The hall tickets must be preserved until the final results of the Indian Forest Service Examination 2024 are announced.

UPSC IFS Mains Admit Card 2024: How To Download? Candidates who will be taking the IFS Mains exam can download the admission card by following the processes outlined below: Step 1 — Visit the UPSC’s official website, upsc.

gov.

in.

Step 2 — Click on the UPSC IFS Mains Admit Card 2024 link on the homepage.

Step 3 — A new page will open, prompting you to input your login information.

Step 4 — Once completed, your admit card will appear on the screen.

Step 5 — Check the admit card and download the page.

Step 6 — Keep a hard copy of the small for reference in the future.

Once you have downloaded the admit card, make sure that all of the information on the e-admit card, such as name, photograph, and other details are correct.

If there is a discrepancy, it must be reported to the appropriate department promptly by email at soexam9-upsc@gov.

in so that the necessary modifications can be done before the examination day.

The UPSC IFS Mains 2024 exam will take place starting November 24.

The examination will span three hours and be conducted in two sessions: the morning session from 9 am to noon, and the afternoon session from 2:30 pm to 5:30 pm.

Candidates are advised that entry to the exam venue will close 30 minutes before each session, and no candidate will be permitted to enter after the entry deadline.

The Indian Forest Service (IFS) Selection Process 2024 consists of three stages: Preliminary, Mains, and Interview.

Candidates must successfully pass each stage to move forward, with the final selection based on a combination of their Mains and Interview scores.
