Ashish Jaiswal: Leading Shiv Sena In Ramtek Assembly Elections 2024

In the political landscape of East Vidarbha, particularly in the Nagpur district, the Shiv Sena has struggled to make significant inroads amidst the dominant presence of Congress and BJP.

However, Ashish Jaiswal has emerged as a notable exception, consistently delivering victories for Shiv Sena in the Ramtek Assembly Constituency since 1999.

His achievements include winning three consecutive assembly elections in 1999, 2004, and 2009 under the Shiv Sena banner, proving his unwavering influence in the region even against the backdrop of BJPs stronghold and prominent political figures like Devendra Fadnavis and Chandrashekhar Bawankule.

Ashish Jaiswal, a figure synonymous with success for Shiv Sena in Ramtek, is once again in the spotlight as he contests from the Ramtek Assembly Constituency in the upcoming 2024 elections.

His political journey has been marked by a unique blend of resilience and adaptability, managing to secure a victory as an independent candidate in 2019 amidst a breakup between Shiv Sena and BJP.

This move demonstrated his deep-rooted influence and the unwavering support of his constituents, regardless of party affiliations.

With such a track record, Jaiswal has rightfully earned the confidence of the Shiv Sena leadership, who once again have endorsed him for the 2024 assembly elections.

Under Jaiswals leadership, the Ramtek Assembly Constituency has seen remarkable infrastructure development, a testament to his commitment to the regions progress.

His efforts have resulted in the completion of two thousand kilometers of rural roads across Ramtek, Parshwini, and Mauda talukas, enhancing connectivity between villages and cities.

Moreover, the construction of a 111 km long asphalt road under the hybrid annuity scheme of the state government further underscores his focus on improving local infrastructure.

These initiatives not only facilitate easier movement but also contribute significantly to the socio-economic development of the area.

Beyond infrastructure, Ashish Jaiswal has also focused on the spiritual and social welfare of his constituents.

By supporting the construction of 64 Buddha Viharas in the region, he aims to foster spiritual sentiments among the local citizens, paying homage to Bharatratna Doctor Babasaheb Ambedkars legacy.

Additionally, Jaiswal has been instrumental in implementing government schemes to provide housing for women in need.

His decisive roles in the Shabari Griha Nirman Yojana and Ramai Housing Scheme have secured homes for thousands of tribal and Scheduled Caste women, respectively, embodying his commitment to empowering women at the grassroots level.

Jaiswals leadership extends to enhancing the constituencys connectivity with pivotal bridge constructions over significant rivers like Kanhan and Pench.

These projects, including the bridges from Waghola to Ghatrohana, Salai Mauli over Pench river, and Dahegaon Joshi to Isapur, not only improve transportation but also signify the strides made in regional development under his guidance.

His aggressive yet constructive approach to leadership has solidified his reputation as a formidable figure in Ramteks political arena.

Amid his various contributions, Ashish Jaiswal has not only fortified his political standing but also fostered strong relationships with influential figures across the political spectrum, including top BJP leaders and the current Chief Minister, Eknath Shinde.

This network of alliances, coupled with his significant achievements, underpins the widespread belief among his supporters that he will once again hoist Shiv Senas saffron flag in Ramtek.

As the 2024 assembly elections approach, Ashish Jaiswal stands as a beacon of hope for his party and constituents, ready to continue his legacy of development and success in the constituency.
