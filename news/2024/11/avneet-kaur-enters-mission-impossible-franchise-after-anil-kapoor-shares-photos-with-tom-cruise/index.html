Avneet Kaur Enters Mission Impossible Franchise After Anil Kapoor? Shares Photos With Tom Cruise

Avneet Kaur shared photos with Hollywood star Tom Cruise on her Instagram handle stoking fan frenzy.

The two appeared to be on set of the next Mission Impossible movie, Dead Reckoning Part 2.

While the Tiku Weds Sheru actress did not confirm or deny playing a part in the next MI film and sharing the screen space with Cruise, many speculated that the upcoming action film will indeed be her Hollywood debut.

If the rumours are true then after Anil Kapoor, Avneet will only be the second Indian star to feature in a role in the legacy film franchise.

Avneet with Tom Cruise | Image: Avneet Kaur/Instagram 'Still pinching myself'Avneet shared that she had the opportunity to visit the sets of Mission Impossible Dead Reckoning Part 2 where she interacted with the franchise star Tom Cruise.

She posted some candid snaps from their catch-up and wrote, "I’m still pinching myself! I had the incredible opportunity to visit the set of the next #MissionImpossible film, starring the one and only Tom Cruise! Witnessing the filmmaking magic firsthand was awe-inspiring.

Tom’s dedication to performing real, practical stunts continues to raise the bar.

Can’t wait to share more about my experience! Stay tuned for updates closer to the release date, May 23, 2025 (sic).

"Tom Cruise on the set of Mission Impossible 8 | Image: Avneet Kaur/InstagramSoon her comments section on Instagram was flooded with congratulatory messages.

"I can't even imagine how happy and proud your mom and dad will be (sic)," wrote a social media user, while another one commented,"Whoa, this is big.

MI movie set with Cruise is legendary (sic).

"Will Avneet follow in the footsteps of Anil Kapoor?Anil Kapoor has played a part in the Mission Impossible franchise.

He featured in a role in the fourth MI movie, titled Mission Impossible: Ghost Protocol (2011).

Parts of the film were shot in Mumbai, with Anil playing the role of Brij Nath, an Indian businessman.
