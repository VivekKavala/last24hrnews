Not Kohli, Ajay Devgn picks two cricketers as his favourites

NEW DELHI: In a recent episode of The Ranveer Show podcast, Bollywood star Ajay Devgn surprised fans with his choice of favourite Indian cricketers.

While host Ranveer Allahbadia labelled the pair of Virat Kohli and Rohit Sharma as an "iconic pairing" of his generation, Devgn went in a different direction, naming MS Dhoni and Rohit Sharma as his all-time favourites.

When asked to choose a duo in cricket similar to his own partnership with filmmaker Rohit Shetty, Devgn responded warmly, saying, “Mujhe Dhoni aur Rohit dono hi bohot pasand hain (I like both MS Dhoni and Rohit Sharma),” highlighting his admiration for both players.
