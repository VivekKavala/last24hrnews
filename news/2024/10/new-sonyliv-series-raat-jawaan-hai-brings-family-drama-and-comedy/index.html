New SonyLIV series Raat Jawaan Hai brings family drama and comedy

The Indian Hindi-language series Raat Jawaan Hai, directed by Sumeet Vyas, premiered on SonyLIV in October 2024.

Produced by Yamini Pictures Private Limited, this new release is headlined by Barun Sobti, Anjali Anand, and Priya Bapat.

Known for its comedic and dramatic tones, the show explores family and relationship dynamics in an urban setting.

Raat Jawaan Hai consists of eight episodes, each spanning 29 to 39 minutes, now available for SonyLIV subscribers.

When and Where to Watch Raat Jawaan Hai The series, available exclusively on SonyLIV, was launched on October 11, 2024.

Its eight episodes are now streaming for SonyLIV subscribers, with episode lengths ranging from 29 to 39 minutes, making it accessible for viewers looking for a drama-heavy series centred on family relationships.
