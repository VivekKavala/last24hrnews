When Aishwarya Rai Opened Up About Her 'Hypersensitive' Side

All eyes have been on Aishwarya Rai Bachchan and Abhishek Bachchan ever since speculations of their divorce have surfaced.

The couple married in 2007, at the height of their careers, and welcomed their daughter, Aaradhya, four years later.

However, more than a decade after their marriage, troubles seem to have struck their lives as the two have refrained from making any appearances together in the past.

Both have chosen to maintain a dignified silence about the situation.

Meanwhile, their fans have been concerned and seeking answers.

Recently, Aishwarya and Abhishek’s fans dug out a video where the Dhoom 2 actress sat for an interaction on the popular chat show Rendezvous with Simi Garewal.

During their conversation, the host talked about the actress’ “two faces,” highlighting that she has seen her public face as “the star” and has also seen her “private face” with her family.

Simi said, “People would be surprised because there’s the glamorous red carpet, and there are simple middle-class values at home,” and asked, “Your parents, they still cringe at scandals, don’t they?” Aishwarya, in a calm and composed way, answered, “I’m sure.

We are all sensitive people.

They get more upset if it hurts me.

” As soon as Aishwarya completed her sentence, Simi played a video of an earlier interaction with the actress.

In the clip, the Mohabbatein actress mentioned that she’s “hypersensitive” and added, “It’s good because it keeps me rooted, but the negative is sometimes it really takes over.

So, while I come across as cool, calm and composed, it’s not a front.

I think that’s a facet to me.

” Aishwarya also discussed that she wanted to be thicker-skinned.

She said, “Sometimes I just wish I was more tough, more thick-skinned, which I am not.

” When the video ended, Simi asked her if she developed a thick skin.

Aishwarya chuckled at the question and answered, “No, I wish.

” On Aishwarya Rai’s recent birthday, the Bachchan family did not share any posts wishing the actress a happy birthday, further intensifying the rumours of trouble in the family.

Even her husband did not share anything on his wife’s 51st birthday.

For the uninitiated, the speculations of Abhishek and Aishwarya’s separation spread like wildfire when the Bachchan family entered Anant Ambani and Radhika Merchant’s wedding venue together.

At the same time, Aishwarya and her daughter Aaradhya arrived separately, raising quite a few eyebrows.
