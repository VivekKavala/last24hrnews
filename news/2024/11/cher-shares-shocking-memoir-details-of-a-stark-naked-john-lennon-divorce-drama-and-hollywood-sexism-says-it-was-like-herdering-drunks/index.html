Cher Shares Shocking Memoir Details of A 'Stark Naked' John Lennon, Divorce Drama, and Hollywood Sexism; Says 'It Was Like Herdering Drunks'

Cher’s latest memoir offers a revealing look into her life, including wild celebrity encounters and a raw, often challenging journey through fame, love, and industry hardships.

Known for her resilience and independence, Cher shares anecdotes that provide fans with an intimate view of her life as a Hollywood legend navigating unforgettable events.

In Cher: The Memoir, the pop icon recounts an incident from 1974, when she encountered John Lennon and his friend Harry Nilsson in a Los Angeles restaurant.

Spontaneously, she invited them to the Playboy Mansion, only to discover that the duo had been drinking heavily.

As the night unfolded, Lennon and Nilsson began calling out to Hugh Hefner in exaggerated accents and causing a stir.

Trying to keep them out of trouble, Cher led them to the mansion’s grotto, which she describes as a cave-like hideaway that connects to the pool.

Upon returning with drinks, Cher was surprised to find Lennon and Nilsson standing naked in the water, laughing and seemingly unbothered by their lack of clothing.

She humorously recalls the challenge of getting them back into their clothes, likening it to “herding drunks.

” The incident, while lighthearted in hindsight, highlights the unpredictability of celebrity interactions in the lavish setting of the Playboy Mansion.

Cher reveals that her daughter Chastity, now Chaz Bono, often visited Hefner’s mansion as a child and even received a life-sized lion statue as a gift from Hefner.

Alongside these unforgettable stories, Cher’s memoir also delves into deeper aspects of her life, addressing her publicized divorces and struggles against sexism in Hollywood.

As a pioneering woman in entertainment, Cher often encountered resistance and scrutiny, which only strengthened her resolve.

Her accounts shed light on the toll of balancing personal life and career while constantly facing societal expectations.

Cher’s memoir is a heartfelt reflection of her resilience and the journey that led her to become a global icon.

Through candid stories and touching memories, she invites readers to witness both the joys and hardships of her life, showcasing her enduring spirit and passion.

This memoir not only celebrates Cher’s unique life but also inspires fans by capturing the wisdom and strength of a star who’s never been afraid to stand on her own.
