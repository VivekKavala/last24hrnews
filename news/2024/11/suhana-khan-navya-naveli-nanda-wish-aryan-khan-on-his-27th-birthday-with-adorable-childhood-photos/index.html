Suhana Khan, Navya Naveli Nanda Wish Aryan Khan On His 27th Birthday With Adorable Childhood Photos

Shah Rukh Khan and Gauri Khan’s son Aryan Khan is celebrating his 27th birthday today.

His fan pages have flooded social media with lovely wishes for him.

On this day, Aryan’s sister Suhana, and his friends often share some lovely unseen pictures of the star kid, and it is an absolute treat for fans.

Now, Suhana and Navya Naveli Nanda have finally wished Aryan on social media.

They took a trip down the memory lane, giving fans a glimpse of Aryan’s childhood through the beautiful photographs.

On Tuesday evening, Amitabh Bachchan’s granddaughter Navya Nanda took to her Instagram story to share a collage of two pictures.

One of them is from their childhood, while another is a recent picture.

The childhood pic shows Aryan Khan sitting next to Navya Nanda.

They seem to be enjoying a birthday party.

Aryan, dressed in a blue sweatshirt and denim jeans, flashes a smile as he holds a candy floss in his hand.

Meanwhile, Navya is seen in a cute white frock as she gorges on cotton candy.

The recent picture shows Aryan with an arm around Navya’s shoulder, while she has a hilarious expression on her face.

“Happy Birthday Aryan,” wrote Navya, with a red heart emoji.

Navya Nanda’s Instagram story For the unversed, Navya Nanda and Aryan Khan are friends, and they attended Sevenoaks School in England together in 2016.

Back then, several pictures of Aryan and Navya hanging out with their school friends had gone viral on social media.

Meanwhile, Aryan’s sister Suhana Khan also shared a collage of two lovely pictures.

The first one is a major throwback picture from their childhood.

Shah Rukh Khan also features in the photo.

Suhana and Aryan are seen making funny faces, while Shah Rukh Khan poses with them.

Another picture is from a photoshoot, which shows Aryan, Suhana and Shah Rukh in outfits from Aryan’s brand.

Suhana Khan’s Instagram story On the work front, Aryan Khan and Shah Rukh Khan have been dubbing for their next ‘Mufasa: The Lion King’.

They have lent their voices to Mufasa and Simba, respectively.

The film will hit the theatres on December 20.

Aryan will also make his directorial debut with the web series ‘Stardom’.
