New Pepe rivals to watch this season—next meme coins to explode?

Pepe is, at this point, one of the meme coin icons.

It has showcased one of the most remarkable performances in the meme coin space.

Soon after its launch in 2023, it became an instant hit, exploding to a billion-dollar valuation.

Pepe’s growth delivered four-digit returns for early adopters.

However, new investors who hope to generate similar profits from the frog-themed coin will likely be disappointed.

Why is the Pepe price slowing down?The PEPE price achieved an all-time high of $0.

00001718 in May 2024.

However, its value has since fallen by around 40%.

The decline signals a shift in momentum.

While altcoins are usually backed by utilities, meme coins lack any relevant purpose.

They depend on hype cycles and community support to generate value.

That, in turn, makes them more vulnerable to rapid price fluctuations.

Pepe is no exception to the trend.

The project is inspired by the internet sensation ‘Pepe the Frog’.

As the website makes it clear, it has no utility.

Although Pepe’s market cap towers above $3 billion now, the meme coin community regards Pepe as lukewarm.

Being largely saturated, it doesn’t have the potential to ignite an explosive journey.

115083696Dogecoin and Shiba Inu have also failed to kindle action.

Their current selling prices are almost 80% down from their all-time highs.

They hold stronger positions due to their brand value but are still being overshadowed by newer meme coins.

Solana-based meme tokens like Bonk and Dogwifhat, for example, shifted attention away from Pepe early this year.

Being purely speculative, Pepe has low appeal among long-term investors.

In a niche where investors are constantly on the lookout for the next big thing, Pepe could further shed weight.

Here, novelty is more prized.

The emergence of meme coins like POPCAT and NEIRO to popularity demonstrates how quickly interest shifts within the market.

The volatile environment is more fertile for newer meme coins, leaving established ones like Pepe struggling to reclaim their past glory.

So, here is a closer look at two new meme coins capturing the market’s attention.
