Golf: Crowd favourite Fowler cards 64 and moves to Top-10 at Zozo Championships

Inzai City (Japan), Oct 25 (IANS) Rickie Fowler, who has Japanese connections, as did defending champion Collin Morikawa and Xander Schauffele had a good outing on the second day of the Zozo Championship in Japan.

Fowler shot 65 and moved to T-7th, while Schauffele, who turned 31 as he played the second round carded 65 and moved up to T-42 and Morikawa scored 67 to be T-22.
