Cars owned by 'The Great Khali': Swanky wagons of the WWE Hall of Famer

The WWE Hall of Famer has been etched in history as the first Indian born World Heavyweight Champion.

Since his retirement from the ring, he has contributed greatly to the development of wrestling in India, and continues to be an iconic presence in the industry.
