Vijay Deverakonda Has This To Say About His Viral Fall Incident

Vijay Deverakonda left his fans worried when he slipped down the stairs just days ago.

The actor was spotted in Mumbai for the promotion of his upcoming music video Sahiba with Radhikka Madan, when the incident happened.

Recently, Vijay reacted to the viral moment with a hilarious video, sportingly taking his “fall” and using it as a clever marketing strategy.

On Sunday, November 10, Vijay dropped a reel on his Instagram account, which opened with the clip where he slipped down the stairs.

The very next moment, he popped up in the frame wearing a green-coloured tee from his clothing brand RWDY.

He paired it with beige trousers and a yellow cap.

As the video played further, Vijay winked at the camera while “Fall, Falling, Falling in Looove with my RWDY boys and girls” was displayed on the screen.

He posted the video with the caption: “I fell & it went crazy ? That is the RWDY life.

RWDYs go all out always.

Irrespective of highs and lows.

& RWDY essentials sell out always.

Lots of love.

Stay RWDY,” followed by a red heart emoji.

View this post on Instagram A post shared by Vijay Deverakonda (@thedeverakonda) Soon, Vijay’s fans flooded the comment section of the post, appreciating the actor’s marketing strategy.

One fan wrote, “That was savage!!!!” Another said, “Hahaha, love this.

Nobody could have done this any better or thought about using the fall video and use it for own advantage.

” A third fan penned, “That’s my boy, fallen hard but came back harder, lessggoooooo.

” On November 8, Vijay Deverakonda was seen in Mumbai with Radhikka Madan and singer Stebin Ben to promote their upcoming song Sahiba.

In a video shared by a paparazzo page, they were exiting a venue when the 35-year-old lost his control, and as he was walking close to the wall without holding the railing, he slipped down the stairs.

His team immediately rushed to help him out and the actor got up with grace while maintaining his composure.

Meanwhile, the singer and other people rushed to cover the cameras of the paparazzi stationed outside the venue.

View this post on Instagram A post shared by Instant Bollywood (@instantbollywood) Speaking about Vijay’s work front, he had two releases in 2024: The Family Star, co-starring Mrunal Thakur, and the multi-starrer sci-fi film Kalki 2898 AD, in which he had a cameo appearance as Arjuna.

At present, Vijay is busy with the shoot of his upcoming action drama VD12, scheduled to be released in the first half of 2025.
