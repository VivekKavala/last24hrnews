Grammys 2025 Nominations: Snubbed K-Pop Acts By BTS, BLACKPINK, Stray Kids Invites Asian Backlash

The nominations for 67th Grammy Awards were announced earlier on Friday.

While fans of Beyonce were happy as leads with 11 nominations, but Kpop fans were angry as their groups BTS and BLACKPINK were snubbed.

Grammys sidelines kpop for nomination of 2025 awards?Grammys had earlier faced accusation for ignoring Asian artists, but fandom of BTS, BLACKPINK, Stray Kids was really left displeased all over again as their favourite K-Pop Band found themselves with no nomination at this year’s Grammys.

BLACKPINK’s Lisa put forward her track New Woman for Record of the Year and Song of the Year, while also vying for Best New Artist and Best Pop Solo Performance with her song Rockstar.

Stray Kids had a strong presence too, with their submissions like Ate for Album of the Year and Best Pop Vocal Album, and Chk Chk Boom for Song of the Year, Record of the Year, Best Music Video, and Best Pop Duo/Group Performance.
