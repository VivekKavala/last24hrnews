iQOO 13 With Snapdragon 8 Elite SoC, 120W Fast Charging Launched

iQOO 13 was launched in China on Wednesday.

The smartphone comes with Qualcomm's latest Snapdragon 8 Elite SoC paired with up to 16GB of RAM and up to 1TB of onboard storage.

The company packs its in-house Q2 gaming chip in the phone as well.

It ships with Android 15-based OriginOS 5 skin on top.

The handset comes with a 50-megapixel triple rear camera unit and a 32-megapixel selfie shooter.

The phone is confirmed to launch in India in soon.
