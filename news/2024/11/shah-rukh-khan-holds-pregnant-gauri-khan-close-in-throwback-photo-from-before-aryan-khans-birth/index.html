Shah Rukh Khan Holds Pregnant Gauri Khan Close In Throwback Photo From Before Aryan Khan's Birth

Gauri Khan’s photo from when she was pregnant with Aryan Khan in 1997 has resurfaced on social media and has caught everyone’s attention.

In the viral photo, Shah Rukh Khan can be seen holding Gauri close as they pose with Chikky and Deanne Panday, who are Chunky Panday’s older brother and sister-in-law.

In the viral photo, Gauri can be seen flaunting a kurta with a red bindi and dark red lipstick as Shah Rukh held her close.

The superstar donned a blue shirt with dark pants.

The image was shared on Reddit with the caption, “SRK, Chikky Pandey, Gauri and Deanne together when they were both pregnant in 1997!!” The image also featured Deanne pregnant with Ahaan Panday.

Check out the photo here: SRK, Chikky Pandey, Gauri and Deanne together when they were both pregnant in 1997!! byu/puckyt inBollyBlindsNGossip Shah Rukh and Gauri have often spoken about her first pregnancy.

In an old interview, Simi Garewal asked Gauri Khan if Shah Rukh Khan shared the pregnancy experience with her.

Gauri said that Shah Rukh tried very hard.

Recalling the time Gauri went into labour, Shah Rukh said, “I didn’t know how a woman reacts when she goes into labour.

I had heard that you’re supposed to breathe with her, but she wasn’t breathing.

Yeah, she was screaming very strangely, nothing like how they show in films.

” Gauri said, “I hardly had any labour pain because I had a Caesarean.

” Shah Rukh added, “Then they went for the Caesarean and it was very stupid because I was like when the baby’s coming out, you’re supposed to breathe.

I didn’t know that it didn’t matter with a Caesarean.

I was still breathing like that.

” Recalling the time of Aryan’s delivery, Gauri said, “He was nearly pulling the baby out.

The doctors were like, ‘Let us do our work.

'” Shah Rukh said, “I went in with my mask and everything and took the baby out.

I saw the incision and everything, though she told me not to see it.

I really enjoyed the moment.

I’m not trying to sound morbid or gruesome, but the sight of this, nature’s colours of even redness of the blood or blue parts there or yellow fat, it’s so translucent.

It’s not at all gruesome.

You’ll never see colours like that in your life.

” Meanwhile, Aryan will soon make his debut as a director with his web series based on the reality of the Hindi entertainment industry.
