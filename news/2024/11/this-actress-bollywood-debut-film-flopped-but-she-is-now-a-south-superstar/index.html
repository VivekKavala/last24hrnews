This Actress’ Bollywood Debut Film Flopped, But She Is Now A South Superstar

The borders between different regional film industries as well as Bollywood have become more faded now.

Many stars have crossed over, trying to make a name outside their native industry.

Actors like Bobby Deol and Sanjay Dutt are now acting in films down South, while actors like Naga Chaitanya, Rashmika Mandanna and Dulquer Salmaan are appearing in Hindi films.

However, a few decades back, these kinds of transitions were not that profound and there was no guarantee of success.

While actors like Kamal Haasan, Nagarjuna and Rajinikanth did appear in some successful Hindi films, there was one actress whom the Hindi film industry had written off after just one unsuccessful film.

However, she went on to be one of the biggest stars of the Tamil industry.

We are talking about Jyothika, who has impressed with her impeccable skills in blockbuster South films like Poovellam Kettuppar, Mugavaree, Star, Chandramukhi, Dumm Dumm Dumm and Khushee.

Jyothika’s sister Nagma was already an established actress in both the Tamil and Hindi industries when Jyothika decided to make her debut.

Jyothika debuted in the Hindi film Doli Sajake Rakhna, opposite Akshaye Khanna in 1997, which was directed by Priyadarshan.

The film, however, failed to make any impact at the box office and critical reception was not too good either.

The setback made Jyothika turn away from the Hindi film industry for a long time and concentrated on Tamil cinema.

In a recent interview, Suriya shared that when he first met his wife Jyotika, she was already earning three times more than him.

He revealed that for the movie Kaakha Kaakha in which both of them starred, Jyothika was paid three times the amount he was paid.

After a very long absence from Hindi cinema, Jyothika recently made a comeback with the supernatural thriller Shaitaan, which also starred Ajay Devgn and Madhavan.
