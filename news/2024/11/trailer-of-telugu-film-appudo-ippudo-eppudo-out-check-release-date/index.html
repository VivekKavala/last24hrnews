Trailer Of Telugu Film Appudo Ippudo Eppudo Out, Check Release Date

Well-known actor Nikhil Siddhartha will make his Telugu debut with the upcoming romantic action movie Appudo Ippudo Eppudo, in which he will be seen playing the lead role.

Sandalwood actress Rukmini Vasant will also be seen to mark her Telugu debut through this film.

The trailer for the film, which was directed by Sudheer Verma, was unveiled recently.

Appudo Ippudo Eppudo will be released in theatres on November 8, Friday.

Nikhil Siddhartha is poised to make an impression with his new movie.

The partnership between Nikhil and filmmaker Sudheer Varma hopes for a hat-trick blockbuster, following Swamy Rara and Keshava.

With this action-packed and exciting trailer, viewers are forming favourable opinions about the film’s subject matter.

Whose corpse is it? What gadget are John Vijay and Ajay looking for as they pursue Rishi? This is the main idea of the narrative.
