Watch: Celebrations Erupt In Usha Vance's Village In India As Trump Clinches White House Race

As the results of the US Presidential Election were announced, celebrations erupted in Andhra Pradesh’s village of Usha Vance, wife of Vice Presidential candidate JD Vance.

Usha Vance traces her roots to Andhra’s Vadluru village in the Godavari district where people started rejoicing and bursting crackers in bliss as the counting of votes went on in the day (Indian time), clearing the picture that Donald Trump would return as the 47th President of the United States.

Who Is Usha Chilukuri Vance? Usha Vance was born to Indian immigrant parents in 1986 in San Diego and was raised in an upper-middle-class suburb.

She has an impressive academic background and works as a corporate litigator at a prestigious firm in San Francisco.

She holds a bachelor’s degree in History from Yale University and a Master of Philosophy degree from the University of Cambridge, according to the New York Times.

Usha Vance’s distinguished career includes clerking for Supreme Court Justices John Roberts and Brett Kavanaugh before Kavanaugh’s nomination to the Supreme Court.
