KL Rahul Shares Goofy Pics To Wish ‘Craziee Birthday Baby’ Athiya Shetty; Ahan Is ‘Grateful’ For His Sister

Actress Athiya Shetty is celebrating her 32nd birthday today, and celebrities such as Kareena apoor Khan, Parineeti Chopra, Ananya Panday, Samantha Ruth Prabhu, and many others wished her on social media.

Her father Suniel Shetty was among the first ones to share a lovely post for Athiya on her birthday.

Now, Athiya’s hubby and cricketer KL Rahul has also shared a romantic, goofy post for her, sharing their unseen pictures together.

Meanwhile, Athiya’s brother Ahan expressed how grateful he is for her.

On Tuesday afternoon, KL Rahul took to his Instagram account to share a series of pictures with his wife Athiya Shetty.

On her special day, the cricketer wished her with a simple yet sweet caption that read, “My craziee birthday baby ,” along with a red heart and infinity emojis.

The first picture seems to be from their pre-wedding festivities.

The actress donned a golden silk saree with a rani pink blouse for their Muhurtham ceremony.

She is seen making a goofy face at her hubby KL Rahul, who is seen holding her in his arms.
