RRB Recruitment 2024: Revised Tentative Exam Schedule For ALP, RPF, SI, Technician And JE Out

The Railway Recruitment Boards (RRBs) have issued the revised tentative schedule for several recruitment exams that are going to take place later this year.

The revised tentative examination schedule has been released for Assistant Loco Pilot (ALP), Railway Protection Force Sub Inspector (RPF SI), Technicians, Junior Engineers (JE) and others.

Candidates who have applied for the above exams can now check the tentative dates by visiting the official websites of the RRB at- rrbapply.

gov.

in.

The exam dates for these recruitment drives were changed on October 24 owing to administrative concerns.

RRBs only included the start and finish dates for these examinations in their updated dates notification.

The official notice states that the dates for further recruitment examinations will be disclosed in due time.

In addition, the exam city information slips and travel authorisation for SC and ST candidates would be made available 10 days before the exam dates for their respective CEN.

Candidates will be able to obtain these from the official RRB websites.

RRB Exam Dates 2024: Tentative Schedule – CEN 01/2024 Assistant Loco Pilot (ALP): November 25, 2024 to November 29, 2024 (CBT-1) – CEN RPF 01/2024 Railway Protection Force Sub Inspector (RPF SI): December 2, 2024 to December 12, 2024.

– CEN 02/2024 Technician: December 18, 2024 to December 29, 2024.

– CEN 03/2024 Junior Engineers (JE), and others: December 13, 2024, to December 17, 2024 (CBT-1).

It is important to note that four days before the exam date listed on the exam city information slips, the admit cards for the four exams will be made available.

“Aadhaar linked biometric authentication of candidates will be done in the exam center prior to entry in the exam hall.

Candidates are required to bring their original Aadhaar card.

Candidates are once again advised to authenticate their identification through Aadhaar verification, if not done already, by logging in with their credentials at www.

rrbapply.

gov.

into facilitate smooth entry at examination center,” the official notice reads.

Candidates are advised to complete the biometric authentication linked to their Aadhar before they enter the exam room.

The RRBs have further notified that watch out for touts who attempt to mislead applicants by making false claims that they would be hired for positions that need illegal consideration.

Computer-based tests (CBT) are used in RRB selections, and candidates are only hired based on their qualifications.
