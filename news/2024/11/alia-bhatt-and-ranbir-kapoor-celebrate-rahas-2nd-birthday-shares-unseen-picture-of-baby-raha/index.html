Alia Bhatt and Ranbir Kapoor Celebrate Raha’s 2nd Birthday, Shares UNSEEN Picture Of Baby Raha

Alia Bhatt and Ranbir Kapoor celebrated a milestone in their family as their daughter, Raha Kapoor, turned two on November 6, 2024.

Social media has been abuzz with well-wishes for the star kid, and fans have been treated to several adorable pictures and messages from her family.

Alia shared a beautiful, unseen picture of Raha as a newborn, cradled in her arms while Ranbir lovingly looked on.

In her caption, she wrote, “2 years today & how I already wish to turn back time to when you were only a few weeks old!!! But I guess that comes with the territory, once a parent all you want is for your baby to stay your baby forever… Happy birthday our life… you make every day feel like birthday cake.

” View this post on Instagram Alia’s post was met with heartfelt comments from family members, including Ranbir’s sister Riddhima Kapoor Sahni, Alia’s mother Soni Razdan, and her Jigra director, Vasan Bala.

Riddhima Kapoor Sahni also took to Instagram to share a picture of Raha with her own daughter, Samara Kapoor Sahni.

Dressed in a white T-shirt, pants, and pink-and-white sneakers, Raha looked adorable, flashing a sweet smile at the camera.

The family gathered for a birthday celebration in Mumbai, attended by close family and friends.

Kareena Kapoor Khan arrived with her son Jeh, and filmmaker Karan Johar attended with his twins, Yash and Roohi.

Ayan Mukerji, a close friend of Alia and Ranbir, was also present to celebrate Raha’s special day.

The couple’s journey as parents began when Raha was born in November 2022, and they gradually introduced her to the public, revealing her face for the first time on Christmas in 2023.

Since then, Raha has often accompanied her parents on special occasions, delighting fans whenever her pictures surface.

View this post on Instagram Ranbir Kapoor is set to star in Nitesh Tiwari’s much-anticipated ‘Ramayana’, alongside Sai Pallavi.

Alia Bhatt, who was last seen in Jigra will soon appear in the spy thriller Alpha.

Both actors are balancing their busy careers with their joyful role as parents to Raha.
