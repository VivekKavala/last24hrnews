With the success of Bhool Bhulaiya 3, let’s revisit the greatest horror content of all times

Bhool Bhulaiya 3 has cemented its position among the top blockbusters this year.

The film has managed to garner over 100 crore collections so far.

We've rounded up five of the most terrifying and iconic horror stories of all time.

These spine-chilling stories have left lasting impressions with their eerie atmospheres, unforgettable characters, and shocking plot twists.

Whether you're a longtime horror enthusiast or new to the genre, these picks are guaranteed to keep you on the edge of your seat.

1.

Yakshini… Ek Dayan (Pocket FM)Kicking off our list is the haunting tale of Yakshini… Ek Dayan is a Pocket FM original.

The show centers around Yakshini, a cursed being of extraordinary beauty who ensnares anyone who crosses her path.

Yakshini terrorizes the villagers of Bangalamuda and Rongkamucha, leading to the sealing of the Graveyard Kothi, believed to be her lair.

For thirteen years, peace reigns, until Yug, a curious local, reopens the mansion, unleashing Yakshini once again.

Written by Anand Usha Borkar, and voiced by a talented cast, including Naaz, this horror drama keeps listeners hooked with its mix of folklore, suspense, and supernatural terror.

Want to know what happens next? Tune into Yakshini… Ek Dayan on Pocket FM and dive into the chilling world of Yakshini.

2.

The ExorcistThe Exorcist (1973), directed by William Friedkin and based on William Peter Blatty’s novel, is a chilling supernatural horror that follows the harrowing tale of a young girl, Regan, who becomes possessed by a demonic entity.

Her desperate mother enlists the help of two Catholic priests to perform an exorcism.

With intense performances by Ellen Burstyn, Max von Sydow, and Linda Blair, the film shocked audiences with its disturbing imagery and realism, sparking debates and even attempts to ban it.

Despite production challenges and rumors of a curse, The Exorcist became a cultural phenomenon and the first horror film to be nominated for an Academy Award for Best Picture, cementing its legacy as one of the greatest horror films ever made.

3.

The Conjuring FranchiseNo horror franchise has captured the supernatural terror of haunted houses and malevolent spirits quite like The Conjuring.

Directed by James Wan, the franchise stars Patrick Wilson as Ed Warren and Vera Farmiga as Lorraine Warren, real-life paranormal investigators who tackle some of the most terrifying hauntings in history.

The iconic characters delve deeper into the haunted objects and cursed entities that have terrified audiences worldwide.

The chilling storylines, impeccable direction, and spine-tingling performances by Wilson and Farmiga make this franchise an essential part of any horror binge-watch.

4.

RaazBollywood's Raaz, starring Bipasha Basu as Sanjana Dhanraj and Dino Morea as Aditya Dhanraj, is a horror romance that left an indelible mark on Indian horror fans.

Directed by Vikram Bhatt, Raaz tells the story of a couple whose marital issues worsen when they move to a house haunted by a vengeful spirit.

The film became a massive success due to its gripping narrative, eerie settings, and memorable performances, especially from Bipasha Basu.

Its combination of suspense, romance, and paranormal activity made Raaz an instant classic, spawning sequels and solidifying its place in Bollywood’s horror hall of fame.

5.

Ragini MMSRagini MMS is a thrilling mix of horror and found-footage style, much like Paranormal Activity.

The film stars Kainaz Motivala in Ragini and Rajkummar Rao as Uday, a couple who stumble upon an abandoned house for a weekend getaway.

What follows is a horrifying encounter with a paranormal entity.

Directed by Pawan Kripalani, Ragini MMS taps into the voyeuristic fears of modern horror, making it one of the most terrifying Bollywood entries in the genre.

The success of the first film led to the creation of the franchise, where Ragini’s ordeal continues, and the scares only get more intense.

If you’re looking for a hair-raising ride, Ragini MMS is a must-watch.

Whether you're a horror buff or new to the genre, these stories are guaranteed to deliver the thrills and chills you crave.

Turn off the lights, grab some popcorn, and prepare for some sleepless nights!.
