Megan Fox, A Year After Miscarriage, Announces Pregnancy With Machine Gun Kelly: 'Welcome Back'

Megan Fox and Machine Gun Kelly (MGK)’s family is getting bigger.

The American actress took to her Instagram account to share the news of her pregnancy with her fans.

The 38-year-old actress is expecting her fourth child; while it is her first baby with her partner MGK.

The happy news comes almost a year after she opened up about suffering a miscarriage.

On Monday, November 11, Megan posted a carousel featuring two pictures on her Instagram account.

In the first, Megan Fox was covered in black oil while she cradled her baby bump.

She tagged MGK.

The next photo was that of a positive pregnancy test, which she proudly showed to everyone.

Sharing the post, Megan wrote, “Nothing is ever really lost.

Welcome back,” followed by a baby face and red heart emoji.

The 38-year-old wants everyone to focus on her pregnancy announcement.

The 38-year-old actress is already a mother of three sons.

Previously, Magan was married to Brian Austin Green.

The former couple exchanged vows in 2010 and had their three sons, Noah, Bodhi and Journey.

They parted ways in 2020.

Meanwhile, Megan’s current partner, Machine Gun Kelly, aka Colson Baker, is a father to his 15-year-old daughter Casie, whom he co-parents with his former girlfriend Emma Cannon.

In June 2020, Megan and MGK confirmed their relationship after the actress appeared in the latter’s music video, Bloody Valentine.

Two years later, the couple got engaged, and Megan shared a video featuring the moment when MGK got down on one knee to propose to her under a banyan tree.

However, earlier this year, the Johnny & Clyde actress confirmed that they called off their engagement and didn’t divulge much details about their relationship status.

Prior to calling off their engagement, Megan and MGK were expecting a child.

Unfortunately, the actress miscarried her baby.

She opened up about her miscarriage, sharing her experience in two poems featured in her book Pretty Boys Are Poisonous.

During a conversation on Good Morning America, Megan talked about the miscarriage, revealing that she experienced such a heartbreaking moment with her then-fiance, MGK.

She said, “I’ve never been through anything like that in my life.

I have three kids, so it was very difficult for both of us and it sent us on a very wild journey together and separately…trying to navigate, ‘What does this mean?’ and ‘Why did this happen?’”.
