BLACKPINK’s Lisa Discusses Solo Career, Fans Slam YG Entertainment Over 'Lack Of Support'

BLACKPINK‘s Lisa recently opened up about her career decisions and her candid remarks are stirring up a storm among fans who are directing their frustrations squarely at YG Entertainment.

On November 13, Vanity Fair released its 2025 The Hollywood Issue, an annual celebration spotlighting the stars of cinema who are creating a buzz in Hollywood.

Among these figures, Lisa appeared on the list, marking her foray into acting with her role in The White Lotus.

In her feature interview, Lisa discussed various subjects, including her bold move to establish her own label, LLOUD, after departing from YG Entertainment.

She reflected on how this step was necessary to fully pursue her solo career ambitions.

“It was a big risk.

I just wanted to have a team that was really focused on me and focused on my solo projects, and have support when I think, ‘Oh, I want to do something,’” Lisa shared.

Her words hit a nerve with fans and they feel her statement highlights a lack of support from YG Entertainment for her solo aspirations.

Fans vented their frustrations, interpreting her decision to leave as a clear indication that YG had stifled her career.
